`tese` exists to serve a specific, real investment move by the owner (Carlos Perche), and the
software roadmap is **paced to that journey** — the tool only needs to exist slightly ahead of the
money.

**The journey (folded in from the owner's research):**
- **Origin:** rotating a small position (~US$ 6k) **out of BTC into global tech equities** in
  2026-06. The BTC disposal is the *cost-base root* of all foreign capital (tracked as
  `btc_origin`; keep monthly crypto sales ≤ R$ 35k for the exemption).
- **Motivation:** wants **autonomy from Brazilian brokers** (Avenue/Nomad/Inter/XP) and **control of
  his own tax record** rather than depending on a broker's informe → hold directly via **IBKR**
  (US + HK/China + KRX). Compared broker costs: Inter cheapest câmbio for small entry; IBKR wins on
  API + global reach (the reason it became primary despite funding friction).
- **Market plan:** US tech as the **core**; China (HK/ADR/Stock-Connect) and Korea (KRX/ETF) as
  **satellites**; ETFs before single names; UCITS flagged near the US$ 60k US-situs estate-tax line.

**About the owner:** senior IC / builder, fully capable of his own investment decisions and stated
so explicitly ("tenho capacidade cognitiva pra isso"). He decides; the tool surfaces data. Chat in
pt-BR; repo artifacts in English.

**How to apply:**
- When building, prioritize what the **next aporte** needs (Phase 1 ledger + manual holdings),
  not the live IBKR feed (Phase 2) — B never blocks A.
- Treat the multi-market + tax-threshold features as **on the roadmap, not v1 scope creep**. See
  [[product-artifacts-handbuilt]] and `docs/roadmap.md`.
